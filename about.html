Who We Are

A collective of experienced designers building a creative studio.

BUILDSPACE STUDIO was founded by experienced architectural and visualization professionals who came together to create a focused, quality-driven studio. We combine technical precision with cinematic storytelling to bring architectural ideas to life in 3D.

From early massing studies to fully detailed interior journeys and 360° experiences, we partner with architects, developers and brands to communicate projects with clarity and emotion.
